Bakngeb River Cave Adventure

Bakngeb River Cave is the attraction in the Municipality of Tboli in South Cotabato. Located in Brgy. Laconon, Bakngeb features a wide river pool where tourists can enjoy swimming with its crystal clear and cold waters. Visitors can also explore what's inside the river cave by riding a raft or go water tubing

Unveiling the Underground

Crystal-Clear Waters: A wide river pool greets visitors at the cave's entrance, inviting you for a refreshing swim in the cool, naturally filtered water.
Subterranean Beauty: As you venture deeper, marvel at the intricate rock formations sculpted by time and water. Spot hidden chambers, glistening stalactites, and majestic stalagmites.
Thrilling Exploration: Choose your mode of exploration! Glide through the cave on a bamboo raft or opt for an exciting experience with water tubing.

Bakngeb River Cave features a wide river pool in its outlet, has naturally cold and crystal clear waters which came from an underground cave system and natural rock formations. The river cave is also home to bats.

How to get there

Bakngeb River Cave is situated in Sitio Bakngeb, Brgy. Laconon, Tboli, South Cotabato. The place can be reached through all types of land transport.

If you are coming from General Santos City, take a bus enroute to Koronadal City. ( Travel Time: 1 hour - 1 hour 30 minutes, Fare: P90- P110)

Then at the bus terminal in Koronadal, take a bus bound for Surallah. (Travel time: 30 minutes, Fare: P28- P35)

At Surallah Integrated Transport Terminal, look for vans bound for Tboli. (Travel time: 30 minutes, Fare at P30-40)

In Tboli, proceed to the Municipal Hall and register at the tourism office. After that, look for a habal-habal (motorcycle) to bring you to Bakngeb. (Travel time: 30 minutes, Fare: P40- P50 per person)

Where to Stay

Tourists who wants to stay overnight in Sitio Bakngeb can bring their own tents and go camping by the riverside or stay at the newly constructed Bakngeb River Cave Receiving Center for only P150 per person, there guests can request for native foods and experience Tboli culture.
