Paraiso Verde Resort & Water Park

Paraiso Verde Resort & Water Park, a world-class tourist destination and the first of its kind in Southern Philippines, offers a fusion of water, sun and fun in one modernized venue designed to fully cater to the needs of both local and foreign guests.

Located in Koronadal City, South Cotabato.

Its four main attractions consist of the Moby Wave Pool, the Adventura River Rapids, the Freestyle Swim Pool, and the Wiggles Kiddie Pool. It also prides itself with a food court and a 300 person-capacity pavilion. It leads in the industry by offering wholesome family leisure in the area.
