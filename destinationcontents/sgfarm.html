SG Farm in Tupi

SG Farm is located in Barangay Kablon, Tupi. From Grand Summit Hotel in General Santo City, it was roughly around an hour and a half drive.

After paying the P100 entrance fee, you leisurely made your way around the wide farm.

Strawberry Guyabano Farm – the name alone evokes an image of colorful fruits and verdant landscapes. Located in Tupi, a charming town in the South Cotabato region of the Philippines, the farm is a remarkable destination for food lovers, horticulturists, and agritourism enthusiasts.

Planning Your Visit to SG Farm

Getting There

SG Farm Tupi, officially known as Strawberry Guyabano Farm, is a serene oasis in Sitio Glandang, Barangay Kablon, Tupi, South Cotabato. The farm is a mere 7.5km from the National Highway, Crossing Kablon, and is easily navigable via Waze or Google Maps. Just set your destination to “STRAWBERRY GUYABANO FARM” in South Cotabato.

Travel Time

From General Santos City, it’s roughly an hour and a half drive to reach this tranquil haven. The roads are mostly cemented, ensuring a smooth journey to your destination.

Operating Hours and Rates

SG Farm is open daily from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM. They offer a variety of options for both day tours and overnight stays. Day visitors can enjoy the farm tour for a P100 entrance fee, which includes trekking and a tour guide.

For overnight stays, there are several options ranging from tent spaces at P500 to the Badjang House for P6000, suitable for 6 pax, including breakfast and entrance fees. Other accommodations include the Open Kubo and two-person setups.

Additional Tips for an Enchanting Experience

Weather and Clothing

The climate at SG Farm can be quite chilly, especially late in the afternoon and in the evening. I recommend bringing a light jacket or cardigan to stay comfortable. Given that it’s a farm tourism site, opt for comfortable footwear suitable for trekking and exploring the vast farm.

Photography

Don’t forget your camera! SG Farm offers breathtaking views of Mt. Matutum and a plethora of picturesque spots for selfies, group photos, and even cool shots on swings and decks.

Dining Options

The farmhouses have charming food and beverage outlets like the Civet Cafe, Rainforest Cafe, and Bayog Bar. These establishments, primarily built with wood and bamboo, offer a range of options from Filipino cuisine to light snacks and beverages.

Accommodation and Amenities

If you’re planning an overnight stay, SG Farm provides various accommodations from modern huts with colorful exteriors to more rustic options. Amenities include toiletries, towels, an electric kettle, water, coffee, hot and cold showers, and charging outlets.

Booking and Peak Seasons

It’s wise to book your accommodation in advance, especially during peak seasons. Keep in mind that the farm can get crowded on weekends, so you might want to consider visiting on a weekday.
